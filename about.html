About Us

We represent "Curly Bracket," a multidisciplinary team proficient in various creative domains, namely photography, videography, video editing, and web development. Currently in our third year of academic pursuit at Phinma University of Pangasinan (Phinma UPang), we are dedicated students enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program, majoring in web development.

Our team is passionate about capturing the essence of moments through the lens, ensuring that each photograph tells a unique story. Our skills extend to the realm of videography, where we adeptly craft visual narratives that engage and captivate audiences. Beyond the art of capturing, we possess a keen eye for detail and creativity in video editing, refining raw footage into polished pieces of visual artistry.

What are we doing?

  • We specialize in capturing moments and emotions through the lens, using our keen eye for composition and lighting to tell unique stories with each photograph.
  • Our team creates visually engaging narratives through videography, seamlessly combining storytelling with aesthetics to produce captivating videos.
  • With a meticulous approach to video editing, we transform raw footage into polished works of art, employing creative techniques to enhance the visual impact and narrative flow.
  • Proficient in programming languages and design principles, we design and develop interactive websites that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing, catering to diverse needs and objectives.
